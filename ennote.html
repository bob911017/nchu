時態

  • 現在式

    • 主詞現在的動作、事實、真理、定律，主詞現在的習慣
    key:現在代未來的各種情況

    現代未的考題:

    1. will VR   →長時間

    2. (is/am/are) going to VR   →短時間

    3. (is/am/are) about to VR   →很短時間

    4. (is/am/are) to VR   →表說話者現在對未來的決定

    5. (is/am/are) + 來去Ving   →極短的時間

    6. ★(is/am/are) on the point of   →表以現在確定未來的計劃表未來 ex:時刻表

    來去動詞用現在式表未來:

    • My father will come back tonight.
      • =My father comes back tonight.

    • My sister returns home next week.

    • He will leave for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.
      • =He leaves for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.
      =He is leaving for Hong Kong tomorrow morning.

    表時間、表條件之副詞子句，動詞用現在式代未來式:

    • If it rains tomorrow, I will stay at home.

    • When he comes tonight, I will tell him the truth.

    • I will write to you after I get to Taipei.

    範例:

    • He usually gets up at six in the morning.

    • He writes to his parents every two weeks.

    • The ROC on Taiwan attend great progress over the past thirty years.
      • 現V +over+T   →從T以來到現在

    • Birds of a feather flock together.物以類聚

  • 過去式

    • 表主詞過去的動作或習慣
    key:兩動作發生的先後比較
    V   ◀────────────▶   T
    Ved 過T +ago
    (have/has) pp since 過T+ ago
    Ved before
    had pp 過T+ before

    ★必考句型: 一 ~ 就 ~

    • 兩個動作發生在過去，一個先(had pp)，一個後(Ved)

      •   S1+ Ved+ as soon as+ S2+ Ved(先)
      =S1+ had+ (hardly/scarcely)+ pp+ before+ S2+ Ved(後)
      =(Hardly/Scarcely)+ had+ S1+ pp+ before+ S2+ Ved
      =S1+ had no sooner pp+ than+ S2+ Ved

    • 兩個動作發生在未來，一個先(will have pp)，一個後(will VR)

      •   S1+ will VR+ as soon as+ S2+ VR(先)
      =S1+ will+ (hardly/scarcely)+ have pp+ before+ S2+ VR(後)
      =(Hardly/Scarcely)+ will+ S1+have pp+ before+ S2+ VR
      =S1+ will no sooner have pp+ than+ S2+ VR

    範例:

    • Columbus discovered America in 1492.
    • They went out just now.
    • I was sick yesterday.
    • He was here an hour ago.
    • He went to the movie once a week when he lived in Taipei.
    • I used to take a walk dinner.

  • 未來式

    • 唯一考題:現在代未來
    key:兩動作發生的先後比較
    V   ◀────────────▶   T
    will have pp for+ T conj(表T)+ 現
    had pp for+ T conj(表T)+ 過

    範例:

    • I shall be eighteen next year.
      • will VR   →不一定會發生
      shall VR   →一定會發生(表事實，被現在式取代)

    • The plane is on the point of taking off.

    • I will have known you for ten years when I marry my wife.
